About Us

Welcome to the Gram Vikas Government Project Portal. Our mission is to empower rural communities through providing details to ongoing development projects, infrastructure improvement feedbacks, and citizen engagement initiatives.

Our Initiatives

We are students from IPM05, who have created a web-based e-government portal called GramVikas, designed to encourage open government, citizen participation, and real-time monitoring of rural development. In addition to empowering residents to report problems, monitoring service availability (such as electricity), and offer feedback on ongoing initiatives, allows regional office administrators to publicise development projects, schemes, and services.
