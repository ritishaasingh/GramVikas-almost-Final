Welcome to GramVikas – Empowering Governance

A web-based e-governance platform designed to promote transparency, citizen participation, and real-time monitoring of rural development projects.

About GramVikas

GramVikas is a digital governance portal aimed at bridging the gap between rural citizens and local administrative bodies. It empowers communities to report local issues, access welfare schemes, and stay informed about ongoing development initiatives. Through transparency, accountability, and citizen engagement, GramVikas redefines grassroots governance.

With a focus on transparency, accountability, and citizen engagement, GramVikas redefines the landscape of grassroots governance.
